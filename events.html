Workshop Highlights

The IIT Bombay–Honeywell Centre of Excellence for Future Skills & Innovation successfully conducted workshop on "Future Skills for Sustainable Development" across four locations in Maharashtra on 28 March 2026. These workshops brought together students, faculty members, speakers, and participants for academic interaction, knowledge-sharing, and discussions around future skills and sustainable development. The students pitched their innovative ideas on various sustainable challanges and its solutions as part of the "Sustainable Innovation Challenge".

Mumbai

The workshop at Mumbai featured active participation of more than 150 students and 12 teams presented their ideas as part of the innovation challenge. Team CampusCart and Team Aquashield emerged as the winners and runners-up for the idea pitching.

Aurangabad

The Aurangabad workshop was conducted successfully with enthusiastic participation and a strong outreach presence.

Kolhapur

The Kolhapur workshop brought together faculty members, students, and speakers for collaborative learning and presentations.

Pandharpur

The Pandharpur workshop included speaker sessions, student presentations, and focused academic engagement.
