About the Centre

The IIT Bombay–Honeywell Centre for Future Skills & Innovation is built to bridge the gap between knowledge and real-world impact. It brings together education, applied research, and industry collaboration to create solutions that matter.

Through structured learning programmes, translational research, innovation ecosystems, and fellowship opportunities, the Centre empowers individuals to become future-ready leaders capable of addressing global sustainability challenges.

Our Focus Areas

The Centre works across four focus areas that strengthen sustainability capabilities, encourage innovation, and create meaningful real-world impact.

Skill & Knowledge Development

Deliver expert-led sustainability learning pathways to build future-ready talent.

Translational Research

Conduct applied research focused on sustainability challenges and practical outcomes.

Innovation & Incubation

Support ideas, startups, and collaborations through a strong innovation ecosystem.

Research Fellowships

Enable fellows to contribute to research, training, outreach, and interdisciplinary work.

Delivering measurable outcomes at scale

The Centre of Excellence aims to accelerate sustainability-driven transformation through skill development, research, entrepreneurship, and ecosystem engagement. It is designed to systematically identify gaps, generate scalable solutions, and drive their adoption across institutions, markets, and communities. By combining capacity building, innovation, and collaborative partnerships, the Centre aims to translate sustainability ambitions into measurable and meaningful impact.
