Skill & Knowledge Development

Deliver expert-led sustainability courses to over 1 lakh learners, building talent and sustainability-driven mindsets at scale. The Centre offers structured learning pathways, hands-on workshops, and interdisciplinary programmes that equip learners with future-ready skills aligned with evolving industry and sustainability needs.

Translational Research

Conduct applied, field-relevant research through research projects focused on contemporary sustainability challenges. These initiatives bridge the gap between academic research and real-world implementation by enabling collaborative problem-solving, data-driven insights, and scalable solutions across sectors.

Innovation & Incubation

Convene national innovation forums to connect students, researchers, industry, and policymakers around sustainability challenges. Support high-potential innovations for startups with mentorship and seed funding to turn ideas into scalable solutions.

Research Fellowships

To be awarded to fellows who will actively contribute to the Centre’s research, training, and outreach components while building their own capacities in interdisciplinary sustainability leadership.
