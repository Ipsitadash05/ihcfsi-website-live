About the Centre

The IIT Bombay-Honeywell Centre of Excellence for Future Skills & Innovation (IHCFSI) has been established to build future-ready capabilities in sustainability through an integrated approach to learning, research, and innovation.

Developed under Honeywell India’s flagship skilling initiative, Saksham, the Centre reflects a shared commitment to strengthening the talent, knowledge, and implementation capacity needed to respond to emerging sustainability challenges.

By combining academic learning with applied research, the Centre supports work in areas such as sustainability assessment, water and sanitation, energy security, sustainable finance, and policy advocacy.

It is envisioned as a platform where knowledge can move into practice-supporting real-world adoption, nurturing skilled professionals, and contributing meaningfully to India’s sustainability transition.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a leading global technology university that provides transformative education to create leaders and innovators and generates new knowledge for society and industry.

Mission

To create an ambience in which new ideas, research, and scholarship flourish, and from which the leaders and innovators of tomorrow emerge. To address problems faced by the nation and the world through the talent we nurture and the research we do. To provide an education that transforms students through rigorous coursework and by providing an understanding of the needs of society and industry. To collaborate with other academic and research institutes around the world to strengthen the education and research ecosystem.
